## Cross-item interpretation

- Q20_1 shows the strongest motivation-driven pattern.
- Q20_2 is also mainly explained by motivation and effect, with support_main operating mostly through the indirect path.
- Q20_3 is the clearest support_main-driven strategic expectancy outcome.
- Therefore Q20_1~Q20_3 should be treated as separate strategic expectancy outcomes rather than a single averaged scale.
